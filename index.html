Engineering Welcome Day 2022

About

Engineering Welcome Day is an orientation event for roughly 700 incoming engineering freshmen and transfer students, hosted by the Engineering Society at UCLA (ESUC). Students will have the opportunity to learn about UCLA's School of Engineering, meet fellow engineering students, hear from engineering faculty, and learn about ther various engineering student organizations and resources UCLA has to offer.

Engineering Welcome Day will take place on September 21 (Wednesday of Week 0) from 9AM - 1PM in the Court of Sciences.

The event will begin with live presentations by administration and staff from 9:00AM - 10:30AM, followed by a chance for new students to get to know their MentorSEAS mentors while exploring the campus. The last activity is an engineering exploration fair from 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM.

The fair will be hosted in collaboration with MentorSEAS and Engineering Ambassadors. MentorSEAS will meet with their mentees, conduct a South Campus Scavenger Hunt, and answer questions from students. Engineering Ambassadors will host walking-tours of UCLA Engineering for attendees to get to explore the School.
