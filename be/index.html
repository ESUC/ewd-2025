About Bruin Entrepreneurs
Bruin Entrepreneurs is UCLA's hub for students of all backgrounds to join in the entrepreneurship community. Our initiatives are available to students with any sort of entrepreneurial drive from weekly speaker events and pitch competitions to a 10-week accelerator program for students to take their startup ideas from ideation to execution. Come see what we can offer you at Bruin Entrepreneurs.
