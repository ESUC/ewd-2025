About Building Engineers and Mentors at UCLA
BEAM is an after-school STEM outreach program that provides weekly hands-on lessons to Title I LAUSD elementary schools to get students excited about STEM. We meet to plan out fun interactive lessons and go in person to the schools to mentor students.
