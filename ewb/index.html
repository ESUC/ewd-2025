About Engineers Without Borders
We are dedicated to helping international communities by engineering solutions and constructing them. Our mission is to support community driven development programs worldwide by collaborating with local partners to design and implement sustainable engineering projects while creating transformative experiences. Use what you learn in the classroom to help communities abroad.
