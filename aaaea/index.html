About Arab American Association of Engineers and Architects
The UCLA student chapter of the Arab American Association of Engineers and Architects (AAAEA) is a non-political, non-religious club dedicated to empower and inspire STEM and Architecture students of Arab heritage to achieve excellence. We do so by organizing both professional and social events for our members.
