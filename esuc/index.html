About Engineering Society at UCLA
The Engineering Society at UCLA (ESUC) is the umbrella organization that aims to build a stronger, ever-improving engineering community by: - Facilitating communication and collaboration across all bodies of the UCLA Samueli School of Engineering - Providing accessible resources for personal, professional, and academic development - Supporting the endeavors of engineering student leaders
